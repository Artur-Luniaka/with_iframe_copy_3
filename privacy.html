Privacy Policy

Your data, your turf – we keep it locked down

What We Collect

When you're in the Gangsta Duel arena or visiting our site, we might collect:

  • Player Stats: Wins, gear, upgrades, and progress
  • Device Data: Like browser type and performance metrics
  • Contact Info: Name and email if you hit us up
  • Gameplay Insights: How you move, shoot, and strategize

How We Use It

We use your info to make sure the streets stay fair and fun:

  • Track your rep and achievements
  • Optimize how the game runs on your setup
  • Reply to your messages and support needs
  • Balance gameplay and keep cheaters out
  • Design new maps, gear, and modes based on player behavior

Sharing the Goods

We don't sell your info – period. We only share it when:

  • You give us the go-ahead
  • The law knocks on our door
  • We're fighting fraud or shady business
  • Trusted partners help us run the game (and sign NDAs)

Security on the Streets

We roll with tough security — encrypted transfers, locked-down servers. Still, no digital hood is 100% safe.

Your Rights, Your Rules

You're in charge. You can:

  • See what we’ve got on file
  • Fix wrong info
  • Wipe your data if you're out
  • Say no to tracking that ain't essential

Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to keep your gear and progress saved. You can manage that through your browser settings.

Underage Gangsters

Gangsta Duel is for players of all ages, but we don’t knowingly collect data from under 13s without the boss’s (parent’s) OK.

If you've got privacy questions, shoot us a message
